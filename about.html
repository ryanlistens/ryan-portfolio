About

I translate complex ideas into clear content. Over the last decade I have created technical documentation for Microsoft supply chain teams, donor campaigns for environmental nonprofits, marketing collateral for SaaS companies, and policy content for criminal justice organizations.

I grew up in the Boston area and built my career there through 2021, working in higher education, staffing, nonprofit advocacy, and consulting. When cost of living became unsustainable, I relocated to Philadelphia and then Buffalo. I am currently in Niantic, Connecticut with family and two dogs.

I make music and do comedy. I write constantly. Out of that passion for creating things people react to, I have built a career in communications. I learn tools quickly, translate organizational missions into accessible content, and adapt to new workflows without friction.

I am looking for work with environmental organizations, mission-driven nonprofits, and companies that need technical documentation, content strategy, or communications support. I have ten years of experience across industries, adapt fast, and deliver work that serves its purpose.
