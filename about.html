About Me

With over 8 years of experience crafting strategic communications, technical documentation, and content marketing campaigns, I specialize in turning complex ideas into clear, engaging narratives for B2B, B2C, and multi-stakeholder environments.

I've worked across SaaS, higher education, staffing, and nonprofit sectors, with a focus on change management, internal/external comms, and process documentation for companies like Microsoft (via RedCloud Consulting), Cambridge College, Hollister Staffing, and Tyler Technologies.

Outside of work, I'm a comedian, musician, and proud dogfather—because creative thinking and humor are essential to both career and life. I value good communication, inclusivity, continuous learning, and meaningful impact.

Based near Philadelphia, PA, but open to relocation for the right opportunity.
